# Light Work Business Proposal

## Working Title

**Light Work Agent Operations**

## Tagline

**Lightweight AI systems for people who do real work.**

Alternative tagline:

**Custom agent operations for small businesses, skilled operators, and creative professionals.**

---

## 1. Executive Summary

Light Work Agent Operations is a service and software venture that helps individuals and small businesses turn modern AI from a general chat tool into a practical operating system for real work.

Most people now have access to powerful AI models, but they do not yet know how to connect those models to their actual business data, documents, workflows, customers, tools, and decisions. Light Work fills that gap.

The company designs, installs, tunes, and maintains customized AI-assisted workflows for skilled service businesses, technical operators, creatives, and small organizations. These systems reduce confusing mental labor, improve follow-up, speed up writing and quoting, organize knowledge, and help one person produce with the leverage of a small team.

The business begins as a productized service: consulting, setup, agent tuning, business-brain creation, and monthly maintenance. Over time, repeated workflows become reusable agent tools, formal dictionaries, source libraries, data schemas, business metrics, and eventually a platform for customized agent operations.

The core promise is simple:

> We turn AI into a working back office for your real business.

## 4. The Problem

Powerful AI tools are widely available, but most people still use them in shallow ways.

Common problems include:

- AI is used as a generic chat box rather than an integrated workflow tool.
- Business information is scattered across emails, notes, spreadsheets, photos, documents, and memory.
- Small business owners repeatedly rewrite the same messages, quotes, proposals, and explanations.
- Leads are lost because follow-up is inconsistent.
- Customers wait while owners manually draft responses or reconstruct job details.
- Experts carry too much operational knowledge in their heads.
- Many AI workflows are expensive because they send too much context, use the wrong model, or lack caching and deterministic tools.
- Prompt engineering by itself is fragile and often disconnected from actual operations.

Small businesses need AI systems that know their services, language, documents, policies, customers, and constraints.

---

## 5. The Solution

Light Work builds custom AI-assisted operating systems for real businesses and skilled individuals.

The service has four main layers:

1. **Workflow Consulting**  
   Identify where time, attention, and information are being wasted.

2. **Custom Local Application Setup**  
   Build lightweight systems around each customer’s real business data and workflows.

3. **Deterministic Agent Tools**  
   Add reliable tools for calculations, lookup, scoring, validation, templates, files, spreadsheets, and databases.

4. **Monthly Agent Tuning and Maintenance**  
   Improve prompts, workflows, source libraries, tools, outputs, cost, and quality over time.

The customer does not buy raw API calls.

The customer buys refined agent work.

### Light Work Local Application Layer

A customized operating layer built around each customer’s tools and habits.

Later versions may use:

### Light Work Agent Tuning

Agent tuning is the process of adapting AI agents to a specific business’s language, data, tools, workflows, standards, and goals.

Agent tuning includes:

- prompt instructions
- examples
- business context
- retrieval rules
- source libraries
- deterministic tools
- output schemas
- workflow boundaries
- model selection
- cost controls
- quality tests
- user feedback loops

Prompt engineering is one piece of agent tuning, but the larger value is workflow design and operational integration.

## 18. Guiding Principles

1. **Do not sell AI hype. Sell useful work.**
2. **Do not replace human judgment. Amplify it.**
3. **Do not overbuild before selling.**
4. **Do not sell raw API calls. Sell refined business actions.**
5. **Use deterministic tools wherever possible.**
6. **Keep systems simple enough to maintain.**
7. **Make AI local to the customer’s real work.**
8. **Measure time saved, follow-up improved, and output created.**
9. **Build reusable knowledge assets without misusing client data.**
10. **Start as service. Productize what repeats. Platform only after proof.**
